How To Submit Picks

You MUST pick 5 games per day (unless there are less than 5 live games that day)
You MAY NOT use College Nicknames (you cannot use Go Dawz as Georgia)
All picks should be submitted in the following fashion:

  1. UMASS +3
  2. FLORIDA -7.5
  3. GEORGIA +20
  4. UCONN -4
  5. STANFORD +10.5

Line Movement

You are 100% responsible for picking the best possible line.
If you enter in a pick in the morning (for example FSU +10, and it moves to FSU +13), you have until tip off to change the line on the sheet.
If someone else on the sheet has the same pick as you with a different line, you are responsible for changing your pick to the best possible line BEFORE TIPOFF.
If the pick locks in and someone has a better line than you, you cannot change it to the better line

Standings Explained

Win % is the determinant of the standings Pushes do not count as losses, and are not used in the win percentge - so pushes are sometimes a good thing and can help you win the week (youll have a higher winnning percentage hopefully).
DNP = Did not pick. DNPs do not count towards your overall standings, but do affect your weekly standings, meaning you cant pick 1 game correctly and then DNP the rest of the week.
DNPs will not affect the overall standings board - just weekly.
